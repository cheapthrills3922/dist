Turmeric

Spices

Turmeric, a plant in the ginger family, is native to Southeast Asia and is grown commercially in that region, primarily in India. Its rhizome (underground stem) is used as a culinary spice and traditional medicine. One of the main components of the spice is a substance called ‘Curcumin” which has potential healing properties. This component is the one responsible for the yellow colour of turmeric.
