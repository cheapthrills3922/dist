Raisins

Type: Dry Fruits

Raisins are processed by drying several types of grapes by different processes. They have a flavor similar to that of grapes, but since they are dried they are sweeter than grapes. Since, raisins are naturally sweet, they are high in sugar and calories, but they also contain fiber, antioxidants, and important minerals like iron.
