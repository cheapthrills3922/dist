About Us

We are GG Traders, your trusted partner in the world of premium spices and dry fruits. We are dedicated to bringing the rich flavors and wholesome goodness of India’s finest produce to kitchens around the globe. GG Traders are a leading supplier and trader of turmeric powder & sticks, raisins, and other spices.

The company was incepted by Mr. Gaurav, a visionary entrepreneur. We are committed to providing our customers with high-quality food products at competitive prices. The company sources its products directly from farmers and suppliers, and ensures that they meet the highest standards of quality and safety. We would like to build relationships where we can be your procurement or purchasing partner in India and you can develop your business in the home country with the best quality products at best rates.

Our Story

Our journey began with a passion for sourcing and delivering the best spices and dry fruits that nature has to offer. We are a start up company in Mumbai. Having experience in real estate sector for more than 2 decades, decided to diversify the portfolio and venture in something new. After studying the import export business market for more than 2 years we decided to start trading in Turmeric and Raisins and have developed connection with farmer manufacturing units and suppliers of both in India.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to provide our customers with the finest spices and dry fruits that enhance the taste and health benefits of their culinary creations. We are committed to sustainable sourcing practices, supporting local farmers, and ensuring that every product we export is a testament to our dedication to excellence.
