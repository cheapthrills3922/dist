About Us

We are GG Traders, your trusted partner in global trade. We pride ourselves on being one of the leading export companies in India, specializing in a diverse range of products and services. GG Traders are suppliers of bamboo incense sticks, turmeric in form of powder & sticks, raisins, and other spices.

The company was incepted by Mr. Gaurav, a visionary entrepreneur. We are committed to providing our customers with high-quality products at competitive prices. The company sources its products directly from manufacturers, farmers and suppliers. We would like to build relationships where we can be your procurement or purchasing partner in India and you can develop your business in the home country with the best quality products at best rates.

Our Story

Our journey began with a passion for sourcing and delivering the best spices and dry fruits that nature has to offer. We are a start up company in Mumbai. Having experience in real estate sector for more than 2 decades, decided to diversify the portfolio and venture in something new. After studying the import export business market for more than 2 years we decided to start trading in Turmeric , Raisins and Bamboo Sticks and have developed connection with farmer, manufacturing units and suppliers in India and across the globe.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide exceptional export services that not only meet but exceed the expectations of our clients. We aim to create sustainable and long-term relationships with our partners by delivering products of the highest quality.
